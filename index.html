Our Story

One Standard.
Every Facility.
Every Component.

Sequoia Automatic was founded in 1958 as a screw machine shop in Crystal Lake, Illinois. Over six decades, we evolved into a global precision machining leader specializing in aluminum thermal management components.

Our strategic partnership with the SV Group — now a full merger after 20 years of collaboration — expanded our manufacturing footprint to Nanjing, China and Chonburi, Thailand. The establishment of Sequoia Precision Components in Saltillo, Mexico positions us to serve the rapidly growing North American nearshoring market.

Today, we serve virtually every global Tier-1 and Tier-2 thermal management supplier in the automotive industry, and we're bringing that same precision to the data center liquid cooling revolution.

Markets We Serve

Two Markets. One Core Expertise.

The same tight-tolerance aluminum machining that manages thermal loads in vehicles is exactly what's needed for the data center liquid cooling revolution.

Automotive Thermal Management

Precision-machined aluminum components for EV battery cooling, power electronics thermal regulation, heat pump systems, refrigerant transport connectors, and traditional HVAC. We serve the world's leading Tier-1 and Tier-2 thermal management suppliers.

Data Center Thermal Management
